What is Student GIG?

Student GIG is a comprehensive learning platform designed specifically for students, beginners, and aspiring developers who want to build real-world skills.

We believe in a learning-first, practice-driven approach. Instead of passive video watching, we emphasize hands-on coding, interactive quizzes, and real projects that prepare you for the industry.
